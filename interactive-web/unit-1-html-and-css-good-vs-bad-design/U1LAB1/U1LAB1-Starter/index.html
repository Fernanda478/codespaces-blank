About Pibble:

Pibble is a name that is used to refer french bulldogs specifically the puppies, while the name is about the breed but this specific dog is the original. There are many variations of pibbles depending on the color scheme of the pibble, white pibbles are called iclouds or gmails but gmails are usually fully grown pibbles, brown pibbles are og(original pibbles) or just normal pibbles, black pibbles are called washington. Pibbles of other colors are just reffered to as pibble, there is also a alien pibble called geeble.
